A little while ago, I became interested in hypercasual games (such as the lengendary Flappy Bird) and thought it would be an interesting challenge to create a game that can be immediately picked up and understood while still being addictive and interesting. So, I designed Space Hopper, a simple mobile game where you control an astronaut drifting through the cosmos.

You can control the astronaut by holding a point on the screen that he will then travel towards. However, he is also influenced by the gravity of the planetoids and black holes around him, so you never quite have full control! This project taught me a lot about mobile development in Unity, as well as working with my girlfriend who created all of the game's artwork.

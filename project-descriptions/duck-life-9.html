At Wix Games, I was an integral part of a small team that created Duck Life 9: The Flock from the ground up. As one of three developers on the team, my responsibilities were many; they included: programming in Unity; writing dialogue for over 100 unique NPC characters and cutscenes; UI programming within Unity; and architecting minigames single-handedly within Unity from open-ended briefs.

The Duck Life series started out with a Flash game on the web roughly fifteen years ago. It was a challenge to design a game that appealed to the nostalgic sensibilities of our long-time fans while also offering a modern, refreshing gameplay loop that stood up against present-day titles. I very much enjoyed being a part of this unique project and we are all very proud of the end result!

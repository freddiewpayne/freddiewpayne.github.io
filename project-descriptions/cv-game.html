When I was entering the Games industry, I wanted to create something that would stand out on my job application, and so I decided to create a short video game all about me and my CV. The game, made with Unity, is easily accessible for anybody and can be finished in a matter of minutes - you can even play it on this website!

It was a challenge to find the right balance between easy and interesting for this game: I know that recruiters often only spend a couple of minutes looking at each application, so I needed the game to be playable in that time while also being fun enough to get me hired! I put a lot of my personality into the game (through dialogue) alongside descriptions of my skillset and experience to give employers a better understanding of me and how I think about video games.

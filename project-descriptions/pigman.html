I created this game as a way of getting to grips with Unreal Engine 5 and the Blueprint visual scripting workflow. I first had the idea for the Pigman game a couple of years before, when I created some assets and began development in Unity - it was very interesting to compare how it went between the two game engines!

The idea I had was all about capturing the feeling of walking through an ancient woodland - I have grown up near these forests and love the unique feeling of exploration and cosiness that only they can provide. Unreal's suite of rendering tools, such as Nanite and Lumen, gave me a great way to try and recreate this feeling with a cartoon aesthetic. My environment design still has a long way to go, but should you wish you can take a look at the project yourself here.
